Privacy Policy

Smart Music Player

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

Smart Music Player respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application uses permissions and how information may be handled while you use the app.

Information We Collect

Smart Music Player does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Media Permission

The app requests permission to access your device's media files so it can:

  • Scan audio files stored on your device.
  • Display your music library.
  • Play music you have saved on your device.

Your music files remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

Advertising

Smart Music Player uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Diagnostic information
  • App interaction data

This information is collected directly by Google and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

You can learn more here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information. Any information collected for advertising purposes is handled directly by Google AdMob.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting user privacy. Since Smart Music Player does not store your personal information on our servers, there is minimal risk of personal data exposure from our services.

Children's Privacy

Smart Music Player is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
